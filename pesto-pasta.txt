Ingredients:
 - Pesto
 - Mixed Veg
 - Chicken cubes
 - Pasta

Directions:
 1. Boil water and add Pasta
 2. Defrost chicken
 3. Chop vegetables
 4. Fry vegetables
 5. Add defrosted chicken to frying pan and continue to fry until cooked
 6. Test Pasta - once ready: Drain and add to frying mix
 7. Add pesto stir through
 8. Serve
